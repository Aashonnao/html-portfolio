Best Movies of all Time

Movies that feels like poetry

Days of Heaven (1978)

An American film directed by Terrence Malick. The film tells the story of a couple, Bill and Abby, who pretend to be siblings and find work on a rich farmer's land. When Bill finds out the farmer is ill, he asks Abby to marry him for inheritance.

Whats Eating Gilbert Grape (1993)

A story about a specially abled child named Arnie Grape, played by Leonardo DiCaprio.

A Walk in the Clouds (1995)

A young married soldier befriends a pregnant lady who is petrified her father will disown her, and agrees to pose as her boyfriend.

The Darjeeling Limited (2007)

Estranged brothers Francis, Peter and Jack reunite for a train trip across India. The siblings have not spoken in over a year, ever since their father passed away. The brothers fall into old patterns of behavior as Francis reveals the real reason for the reunion: to visit their mother in a Himalayan convent.
